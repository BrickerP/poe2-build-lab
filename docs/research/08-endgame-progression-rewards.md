# Finding 08: Endgame progression and reward clarity are high-value guide topics

## Research result

Return of the Ancients reworked the endgame and introduced a large amount of new progression. This creates guide demand beyond builds: Atlas pathing, league mechanics, bosses, reward farming, waystones, and what to do after campaign.

## Evidence

Official live thread says Return of the Ancients includes new endgame storylines, 15 bosses, four pinnacle encounters, new crafting systems, and progression systems.

Official hotfix notes in the live thread repeatedly mention progression blockers and endgame fixes:

- Expedition boss spawning
- Abyss quest progress
- Trial of Chaos access
- Ritual progression
- map/quest completion credit
- waystone sustain from map bosses
- Atlas passive node problems

0.5.2 patch notes also include endgame changes, Atlas passive changes, boss/ritual/delirium changes, and controller endgame map UI improvements.

## User pain

After campaign, players need:

- What should I do first in the new Atlas?
- Which league mechanic should I focus?
- Which questlines unlock passive points or bosses?
- Why did a quest not progress?
- Which rewards are worth farming?
- What changed in 0.5.2?

## Product implication

The site should add an Endgame Hub:

- Atlas first-steps guide
- mechanic-by-mechanic guide: Breach, Delirium, Abyss, Expedition, Ritual, Runes of Aldur
- boss unlock tracker
- waystone sustain guide
- "known bug/progression blocker" tracker sourced from official hotfixes
- reward table by mechanic and map tier
